About Me

Hi, I'm Ramona Stefanescu , and I work as a self-driving car engineer at a startup company in Palo Alto, CA. I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in Computational Mechanics at University at Buffalo and B.S. in Computer Science and Mechanical Enginering at Politechnica University, Bucharest. During my PHD, I have pioneered a framework for using ”Big Data” from high fidelity simulations and innovative statistical analysis designed for large datasets and distributed systems.

My industry experience is in probabilistic robotics research and development, advanced system analysis and control with applications to self-driving cars. I am also pursuing a graduate certificate at Stanford in Artificial Intelligence.
